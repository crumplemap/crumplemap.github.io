Introducing the crumplemap!

Crumple map, reinventing the paper map. The world’s lightest, most durable, soft and weather resistant map which can be squashed, squeezed and scrunched. And what’s more when you return home you are reminded of the magical memories of your adventures.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the map made of?

The map is made out of a high quality microfibre. Perfect for cleaning lenses of cameras and those essential sunglasses for when your visiting Sydney.

What is it a map of?

The map is of the central business district of Sydney, it highlights key tourist sites that are essential when you’re visiting Sydney for the first time!

What language is the map in?

The map is in English.

Will I be able to get this product for other countries?

At the moment we are focusing on Sydney as our founding city, however we are willing to expand our horizons with time.
